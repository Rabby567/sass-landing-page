Common questions

If you don't find your answer here, our team replies in under 2 hours.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — all Pro features are free for 14 days, no credit card required. You'll get full access to AI predictions, unlimited dashboards, and all integrations during the trial.

How does NexusAI handle data privacy?

We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Your data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never sell or share data with third parties. Enterprise plans support custom data residency in the EU or US.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Monthly plans can be cancelled any time from your dashboard with no penalty. Annual plans include a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not fully satisfied.

What integrations are supported?

We support 200+ integrations including Stripe, Shopify, Salesforce, HubSpot, Segment, PostgreSQL, BigQuery, Snowflake, Slack, and more. If you need something custom, our REST API and webhooks have you covered.

How accurate are the AI predictions?

Our churn models average 89% accuracy across all customer segments, and revenue forecasts have a median error of ±4.2%. Accuracy improves over time as the models learn your specific business patterns — typically within the first 30 days.
